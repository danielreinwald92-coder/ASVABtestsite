No Calculators Allowed

Just like the real ASVAB, calculators are not permitted during this test. You may use scratch paper for calculations.

How This Test Works
  • You must answer every question before you can move to the next one — just like the real computer-based ASVAB.
  • Each section has its own timer. When a section's time runs out, the test automatically moves to the next section.
  • You can't return to a finished section. Within the current section you can revisit questions you've already answered.
  • Unanswered questions count as wrong if time runs out.
  • If you switch to another tab, the timer pauses — you won't be penalized for interruptions.
  • Your results appear immediately after the test. You don't need an account, but if you log in first your results are saved to your dashboard.
  • No timer, no pressure. Tutor mode is untimed practice.
  • You get instant feedback and an explanation after every answer.
  • You can move freely between all questions.
  • Tutor sessions don't produce an AFQT estimate — take a timed test for that.

On a keyboard: press A–D to answer, arrow keys to move, F to flag a question.
